About RED Hope

RED Hope is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting patients with rare blood disorders. Our mission is to provide resources, support, and hope to individuals and families affected by these conditions.

We connect donors with recipients, provide educational materials, and offer emotional support through our dedicated helpline and community events.

Your participation in our donor program helps save lives and brings hope to those in need. Every donation makes a significant difference.
